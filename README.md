# ZenChain - Wellness and Mindfulness Tracking System

A blockchain platform for tracking wellness activities, rewarding mindfulness practices, and managing holistic health development.

## Features
- Wellness session tracking and rewards
- Mindfulness level progression system
- Wellness challenge management with time incentives
- Health achievement publishing and validation
- Wellness coaching and mentorship programs

## Smart Contract Functions
- Track wellness and mindfulness sessions
- Manage wellness challenges with completion rewards
- Publish health achievements with community validation
- Provide wellness coaching to community members
- Claim wellness and mindfulness rewards

## Usage
Promotes holistic wellness through blockchain-verified activity tracking and mindfulness achievement rewards.
